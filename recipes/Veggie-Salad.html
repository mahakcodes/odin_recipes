Veggie Salad

Description:

Veggie salad is a fresh, colorful, and healthy dish made with a mix of crunchy vegetables and a light dressing. It’s quick to prepare, packed with nutrients, and perfect as a side dish or a light meal on its own.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup chopped carrots
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • ½ cup sweet corn (boiled)
  • Lettuce or spinach (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander

Steps:

  1. Prepare the veggies: Wash and chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix everything: Add cucumber, tomatoes, carrots, onions, sweet corn, and greens in a bowl.
  3. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, mix lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Combine: Pour the dressing over the veggies and mix gently.
  5. Add extras: Sprinkle chaat masala or coriander if you like
  6. Serve fresh: Enjoy immediately for the best taste and crunch.
