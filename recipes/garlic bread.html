Garlic Bread

Description:

Garlic bread is a warm, crispy, and flavorful side dish made by spreading a garlic-butter mixture over bread slices and toasting them until golden. It’s quick to make and pairs perfectly with pasta, soups, or any comfort meal.

Ingredients:

  • Bread slices or a baguette
  • 3–4 tablespoons butter (softened)
  • 3–4 garlic cloves (finely chopped or crushed)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh or dried parsley
  • A pinch of salt
  • Chili flakes
  • Grated cheese for cheesy garlic bread

Steps:

  1. Prepare garlic butter: Mix softened butter with crushed garlic, parsley, salt, and optional chili flakes.
  2. Spread the mixture: Apply the garlic butter evenly on the bread slices.
  3. Toast the bread:
    • On a pan: Cook on low heat until crisp and golden.
    • In an oven: Bake at 180°C for 8–10 minutes.
  4. cheesy version: Sprinkle cheese on top and bake until melted.
  5. Serve hot: Enjoy alongside pasta, soup, or as a quick snack.
