Pancakes

Description:

Pancakes are a light, fluffy, and comforting breakfast favorite made with a simple batter of flour, milk, eggs, and sugar. They cook quickly on a hot pan and turn golden brown, making them perfect for a warm, homemade treat. Enjoy them with butter, honey, syrup, or your favorite toppings for a delicious start to your day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter or oil
  • A pinch of salt
  • Vanilla essence
  • Chocolate chips or fruits

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add wet ingredients: Add the milk, egg, and melted butter to the dry mixture.
  3. Whisk the batter: Mix gently until everything is combined. Small lumps are okay—don’t overmix.
  4. Heat the pan: Warm a non-stick pan on medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour the batter: Pour a small ladle of batter onto the pan to form a circle.
  6. Cook the first side: When bubbles appear on the surface and the edges look set, flip the pancake.
  7. Cook the other side: Cook until golden brown.
  8. Serve: Stack the pancakes and serve hot with syrup, honey, butter, or your favorite toppings.
